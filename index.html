Engine Policies & Recommendation Criteria

1. Algorithmic Neutrality

Our matching metrics operate purely on telemetry-free logical scoring parameters. We do not accept sponsorships from any distribution development team. Recommendation weights are calculated dynamically in real-time based strictly on performance benchmarks, packages freshness, and configuration stability.

2. Privacy & Sandboxing Policy

All questionnaire evaluation runs and dual-boot partitioning layouts are processed directly within your local client sandboxed storage environment. No metrics, specifications, GPU types, or partition plans are transmitted, stored, or processed on external tracking servers.

3. Gemini API Gateway Rules

The Systems Architect AI chat interface establishes an encrypted, direct-to-destination connection with the Google Gemini API. Your conversation logs are handled client-side and do not undergo intermediate logging. However, users should refrain from supplying root terminal secrets or private SSH credentials during configuration prompts.
